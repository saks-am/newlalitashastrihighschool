About School

New Lalita Shastri High School

The educational Institution "New Lalita Shastri Montesson School Lucknow was established in the year 1989 in the memory and honour of Late Smt Lalita Shastri and her husband our Ex-Prime Minister Late Sri Lal Bahadur Shastri The institution was established as an ideal school with the aim to educate the poor and suffering children of all religions with out any difference. In the beginning the institution was running in a rented building in its own limited resources and a very few children were stuyding.

Due to great efforts of the Managing Committee, the Principal and teachers as well the institution is going on progress by leaps and bounds. At present the institution is running in its own building and healthy environment for students in which all facilities for the mental & physical development for growing children are available The school is affiliated to UP. Board and since the affiliation many batches of student have passed out in flying colours.
